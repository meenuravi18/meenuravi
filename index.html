Results of VARMAX model to predict visitorship from Kings and Sequois National Park
If you plan to go to California for your next vacation, Would you visit not only Yosemite but also Sequoia and Kings Canyon? Or, do you think that if you can enjoy the nature in Yosemite, you don't need to visit the surrounding parks?
